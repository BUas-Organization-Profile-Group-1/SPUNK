The bracelet that brings relief

As a parent, the number one rule is to always watch your kid to avoid danger. Keep your child close, look out for any objects that might be a hazard, don’t approach strangers or run towards the road and more. Wouldn’t you like to feel more relaxed while doing all these routines? Maybe even doing these routines less? Then Valorous has the solution to your problems introducing the bracelet that provides relief. We as a brand want you to feel relaxed as a parent while spending time with your child. With our optimized bracelet observer, you as a parent can always keep track of your child's safety. Bracelet observer has set-in features to track your child’s heart rate, location and build-in mood communication through hourly emote checkups. All with easy access to your mobile device you can receive notifications, check-in and more.
